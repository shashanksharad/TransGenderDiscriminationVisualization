Prologue

This data visualization exercise about transgender discrimination is both saddening & hopeful at the same time. Anybody reading this has my humble request to take a moment to think and realize that we belong to the same social setup that has historically been very unjust, discriminatory and often violent to the transgender community and thus all of us owe our contributions towards creating a better world for them to live.

Transgender Discrimination

Transgender people have been neglected throughout the world for a long time. Except for few countries, there is no recognition of their rights and they are bound to live very miserable lives with widespread discrimination in social, political, economic and educational spheres. In Indian society where I come from, this sexual minority has predominantly been forced into begging or prostitution. As a kid I was taught to despise them and have prejudices without any scientific reason. They have faced social and economic alienation due to discrimination based on their gender identity or expression. 
Lack of sufficient data on their discrimination has further hampered substantial improvement in transgender equality through government policies. Through this work, I've tried to compile & visualize some transgender discrimination survey data and thus highlight the extent of wrongdoings that they have to go through just because of how nature wanted them to be born as.
